Welcome to MetaDome!

MetaDome is an easy-to-use web service that allow you to analyse human proteins for tolerance per-position to population-based genetic variation present in gnoMAD and annotates known pathogenic variation from the ClinVar resource. Furthermore, homologous domain relations are used obtain a much greater wealth of information for positions that fall in a protein domain which has homologue occurrences in human proteins.

Input your gene name and pick your transcript and MetaDome will collect and combine available information to produce a tolerance landscape, annotate PFAM domains and pathogenic ClinVar variants, and provide meta-domain variant annotation. Complete with results, ability to edit and download all the information.
