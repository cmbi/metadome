Privacy

How private is any data I enter in MetaDome?
MetaDome does not save any data that a user may provide. The visualization of MetaDome occurs client-side (in your local browser) and is not accessible to us. This means we can not know which genes, transcripts, domains, and positions you are interested in.
Does MetaDome use personalized tracking?
MetaDome makes use of Google Analytics to help us analyze how users navigate the various site-pages. Google Analytics provides us with anonymized and global statistics such as traffic per country and time of day. We do not employ any cookies or personalized tracking.

We will never (and will not allow any third party to) use the statistical analytics tool to track or to collect any Personally Identifiable Information of visitors to our site. Google will not associate your IP address with any other data held by Google. Neither we nor Google will link, or seek to link, an IP address with the identity of a computer user.
