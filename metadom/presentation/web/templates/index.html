Welcome to METADOM!

METADOM is an easy-to-use web service that allow you to analyse human proteins on positional tolerance to population-based genetic variation present in the gnoMAD dataset, annotates known pathogenic variation from the ClinVar resource, and allow use of homologous domain relations to analyse variant occuring at homologous domain positions in other human proteins.

Input your gene name and pick your transcript and METADOM will collect and combine available information to produce a tolerance landscape, annotate PFAM domains and pathogenic ClinVar variants, and provide meta-domain variant annotation. Complete with results, ability to edit and download all the information.
